Whether it's the soft hues of a sunset, the vivid shades of a blooming flower, or the serene tones of a forest, this new way of makeup ensures your look is always in harmony with the world around you. Embrace the art of natural beauty and let ChromaBlend bring the colors of nature to your face.


  • This project originated from a single question that popped into my head as I was strolling down the street,
    mesmerized by the vibrant hues of nature.
    I pondered, "What if I could infuse the color of nature into my make-up directly? How can I extend nature into my body?" .
