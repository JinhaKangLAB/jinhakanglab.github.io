Jinha Kang is an award-winning multidisciplinary designer & art director based in New York City—the city where creativity never sleeps.
With experience across fast-paced newsrooms, cutting-edge tech, and global ad agencies, she blends aesthetics with strategy to create work that’s as impactful as it is inventive.
She holds an MFA in Digital + Media from Rhode Island School of Design (RISD) and graduated as valedictorian from Seoul National University.
Jinha’s mission? To be the bridge that unites art, design, and technology into meaningful, human-centered experiences.
